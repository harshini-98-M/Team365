TEAM 365

TEAM 365 was founded by Textile Professionals with Rich Corporate & Project Management Experience in 2005.

TEAM365 is a Consultancy Firm to provide Project Consultancy and Project Management & Coordination Services.

TEAM 365 also provides Erection Services, Technical Audits, AMC and Maintenance Services, all under one Roof. It is a One Stop Solution.

Unlike other Consultants, who are essentially One Man Show, A team of 300 Textile Professionals and Textile Technicians work in our team.

Team 365 is the Largest Team in the Country and is the No 1 Textile Consultancy Firm which provides all technical services to Textile Industry.

TEAM 365 was founded with a Mission to provide Professional Technical Services to the Textile Industry in 2005 as there were no Professional Organizations to cater to the needs of the Textile Industry, TEAM 365 has over 400+ Satisfied Customers both in India and Abroad. In the long journey, we have created a lot of Bench Marks in the Industry.
